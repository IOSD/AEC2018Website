about

WE AT IOSD, BELIEVE THAT ENTREPRENEURSHIP IS AN ART NOT A SCIENCE. AMATEUR ENTREPRENEURSHIP CHALLENGE IS ORGANISED ANNUALLY BY INTERNATIONAL ORGANISATION OF SOFTWARE DEVELOPERS TO PROMOTE THE SPIRIT OF ENTREPRENEURSHIP IN HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS. EACH YEAR, 1000+ HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS FROM 100+ SCHOOLS JOIN AEC TO COME UP WITH INNOVATIVE BUSINESS IDEAS. WINNERS FROM THIS CHALLENGE GAIN DIRECT ENTRY INTO GLOBAL ENTREPRENEURSHIP CHALLENGE BY IOSD AND A HUGE SEED FUNDING TO WEAVE THEIR DREAMS INTO REALITY.

23 July

Delhi Public School, R.K. Puram
