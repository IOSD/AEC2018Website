about

WE AT IOSD, BELIEVE THAT ENTREPRENEURSHIP IS AN ART NOT A SCIENCE. AMATEUR ENTREPRENEURSHIP CHALLENGE IS ORGANISED ANNUALLY BY INTERNATIONAL ORGANISATION OF SOFTWARE DEVELOPERS TO PROMOTE THE SPIRIT OF ENTREPRENEURSHIP IN HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS. EACH YEAR, 1000+ HIGH SCHOOL STUDENTS FROM 100+ SCHOOLS JOIN AEC TO COME UP WITH INNOVATIVE BUSINESS IDEAS. WINNERS FROM THIS CHALLENGE GAIN DIRECT ENTRY INTO GLOBAL ENTREPRENEURSHIP CHALLENGE BY IOSD AND A HUGE SEED FUNDING TO WEAVE THEIR DREAMS INTO REALITY.

23 July

Delhi Public School, R.K. Puram

challenge the tracks

B-PLAN COMPETETION

The track is all about making money. Students need to come up with efficient and profitable bussiness plans. Mentors and investors will guide students to continuosly improve their bussiness plan before the showdown.

SOCIAL ENTREPRENEURS

This tracks looks out for candidate social entrepreneurship ventures. Best for students willing to make this a world a better place for the society. Teams will be guided by NGO Volunteers and leaders throughout the competetion.

INNOVATIVE PROJECTS

This track is to support innovative non-technical / technical projects. If the students have worked on any innovative project or models, they can now reach out to billions. Winners will be considered under IOSD'S Dream project initiative.
